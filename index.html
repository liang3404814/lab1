Master Yoda

Yoda is a character in the Star Wars universe. In the original films, he trains Luke Skywalker to fight against the evil Galactic Empire. In the prequel films, he serves as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order and as a high general in the Clone Wars.

Yoda makes his first film appearance in The Empire Strikes Back, then in Return of the Jedi, The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, and lastly in Revenge of the Sith.
